À propos de nous

The Best of Both Worlds — En Français

Private tutoring can be costly, and traditional camps often lack meaningful academic focus. Our French Immersion Summer Learning Program gives your child both: real academic growth, afternoon enrichment, and full French immersion — without the private tutoring price tag.

With just 5 students and 2 certified teachers, every child gets the personalized attention they deserve. Lessons are grounded in the Science of Reading and delivered using a multi-sensory approach — all in French, all day long.

Le Programme

Morning Academics + Afternoon Enrichment

Each day is split between focused academic learning in the morning and creative, active enrichment in the afternoon — entirely in French.

Matin — Morning Focus

Literacy & Reading

Reading, writing, and phonics instruction grounded in the Science of Reading. Students build confidence through hands-on, multi-sensory lessons.

Numeracy & Math

Number sense, problem solving, and math fluency through interactive games and activities that make numbers click.

Après-midi — Afternoon Enrichment

Art

Drawing, crafts, and visual projects that spark creative expression and imagination.

Physical Education

Team games, movement activities, and daily active play that keeps bodies strong and minds refreshed.

Outdoor Exploration

Nature-based curiosity, outdoor observation, and discovery activities beyond the classroom.

Music, Drama & Dance

Performing arts, rhythm, movement, and storytelling that build confidence and self-expression.

STEM

Hands-on experiments, building challenges, and curiosity-driven science and engineering activities.

Pour les parents

A Free Academic Assessment — Included with Both Weeks

When you register for both sessions, your child receives a complimentary academic assessment before camp begins. This is a professional evaluation — the same kind our certified specialists use in school — that helps us tailor the program specifically to your child.

The assessment covers:

  • Reading level — current level, strengths, and next steps
  • Math fluency — number sense, gaps, and grade-level benchmarks

Results are shared with parents and used to personalize your child's experience from day one. The assessment is conducted before camp starts — register for both weeks to receive it at no extra cost.
